Futurelean Clinical Bioinformatics-week3

记录自己在学习《Clinical Bioinformatics》的点点滴滴思考。这次记录的是第三周的课程。

week3 3.7

The 1000 genome project is a study which is attempting to characterise normal variation in the human population. A description of the project and what it has achieved so far can be seen at their web site IGSR The International Genome Sample Resource.

原来1000 genome是对正常人进行测序，获得背景信息，有效变异就能准确显示出来。

in-house databases 自创数据库

EXAC 项目测了60,706个外显子

We normally work on the assumption that any variant seen in more than 1% of a population is not likely to be disease causing

这个是评判一般突变的假设前提。

week3 3.8

这节课是一个讨论课，问题是：

Data centres have a lot of rich data related to specific conditions.

In your opinion should genome centres be encouraged to share their own in-house data?

What would be the risks and benefits of sharing this data?

先说下自己的观点，如果不是拿政府的钱做的数据，该测序中心完全可以自己占有。如果公开很好，但是要保证质量。
下面的讨论也有人说了，对于保险等敏感信息要记得脱敏。

看了下讨论，里面的人都是要求数据公开，不知道是自己思想是不是不够open？

week3 3.9

这节的主题是 Focus on key areas of the genome

There are a number of different places in which we can find panels of genes known to be involved in disease – we can focus our attention just on the genes known to be involved in the disease process as those are the ones we are most likely to be able to report back to the clinician.

讲出了临床医生的心声啊，你给我整那么多突变干啥，各种不确定，我需要的是确定的关联关系，否则就是浪费我本来就不多的精力。

week3 3.10

讨论课，题目是：

What are the pros and cons of using panels of genes in diagnosis?

这个烂大街的话题了吧，目前的情况对于遗传性比较高的基本，就用特定的panel做就好了。当然，如果是以科研那么有钱的话，搞搞外显子或者全基因组。现在有个话题就是，你是否需要花高价得到以后可能以非常便宜的价格就能买到的数据？仁者见仁，智者见智。当你容易买到了，对于科研也就水涨船高了。
评论里比较有意思的：

GREEN gene panels include all genes with a known association to a condtion, whilst AMBER gene panels include additional genes with a proposed link to the condition but are not yet fully establish - I believe this is part of the PanelApp service Ang mentioned

有意思，GREEN gene panel和 AMBER gene panel

week3 3.11

讨论了下数据库。
dbSNP

This is one of the oldest variation databases and captures data from a range of species (not just human) and creates specific identifiers for them – identifiers that are widely used in clinical bioinformatics to describe variants. A first stage in this process is therefore to see if there is a dbSNP identifier for the observed variant – known as reference SNP cluster IDs.

原来dbSNP 存的东西不只是human的。。。

Leiden Open Variation Database，没听说过

This is a specialist databases that can give more specific disease specific. It contains data on variants that have been seen to play a role in different genetic diseases. This is regarded in the community as high quality data that is well-curated by experts

高质量数据库啊。

ClinVar 变异和phenotype有联系。
https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/24234437

week3 3.12

将了变异可能导致的危害，首先是无义突变 就是提前终止。
也讲了 SIFT和Polyphen，下面是吐槽：

However, although the information they provide is helpful, it is not enough on its own to be able to make a clinically reliable diagnosis.

末尾有句吐槽挺有意思：

The only information that can be used for clinical diagnosis is published data that specifically links the particular variant to the disease.

果然现实主义的医生。
