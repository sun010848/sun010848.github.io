这一节的题目是：What is ethics and governance? 伦理和监管？

WHO:

Ethics is concerned with moral principles, values and standards of conduct. The field of health and health care raises numerous ethical concerns, related to, for example, health care delivery, professional integrity, data handling, use of human subjects in research, and the application of new techniques, such as gene manipulation.

世卫组织发话了。

>
“A framework through which NHS organisations are accountable for continually improving the quality of their services and safeguarding high standards of care by creating an environment in which excellence in clinical care will flourish”.

This definition is intended to embody three key attributes:

  • Recognizably high standards of care, 必须是高质量
  • Transparent responsibility and, 职责明确
  • Accountability for those standards, and a constant dynamic of improvement. 明确标准的问责界限。

week4 4-6

题目叫： What is the difference between Privacy and Confidentiality?
什么是隐私，什么是机密？

看定义：

Privacy is the right of the individual to keep his or her health/genomic information private.

隐私是个人保持自己的健康或者基因组信息的私人性质。

Confidentiality refers to the duty of anyone entrusted with health/genomic information to keep that information private.

机密性是指，委托人的健康或者基因组信息的私密性。

隐私强调的是个人，机密强调的是数据。
个人的与研究无关的信息是隐私，个人的数据只能被特定的人看到，是保密。

week4 4-9

什么是 Medical Consent？

To enable patients to have control over their lives and their medical treatment, all patients have the right to refuse medical treatment so long as they are competent to make this decision. Thus, before any medical treatment can be undertaken valid consent needs to be obtained. This is the same for using their genomic data as well.

为了让病人能够自己掌控自己的生命和医学治疗，所有的患者有权拒绝一些治疗，除非他们同意。在任何治疗前，必须获得患者的同意书。对于他们自己的基因数据也是同样适用。

When a person signs the consent form to use their genomic data for research this means that they have decided to take part in a research project. However ‘informed consent’ is about more than just signing a form, it is a process of information sharing and dialogue between the potential participant and the clinical team. This dialogue does not end when the person joins the project, but continues throughout their time as a participant/patient.

当患者签署了同意书同意其基因组数据进行研究时，这意味着他们同意参与该研究项目。知情同意并不只是签署个表格那么简单，而是在信息共享和对话过程中，这一过程并不只是在他们同意参与项目时就结束了，而是在整个过程一直会持续下去。

Taking informed consent means that a trained health professional has explained what participation involves. They are responsible for making sure that the potential participant has a clear understanding of the risks and benefits of taking part and what they are being asked to take part in.

签署知情同意后意味着受训人员以及给参与人员进行解释了整个过程。这些专业人士有责任让参与人员很清楚的了解到在整个过程中的风险和获益，包括参与人员所要参与的内容。

Adult Cancer Consent Form.
Adult Cancer Information Sheet.

You will see the depth of detail within the information sheet to ensure the patient is fully aware of the project and its purpose as well as how their data will be used and how these documents begin the dialogue between clinician and patient.

从上面的两个文档，你可以看到一个很详细的说明表格，确保患者能够完全的了解项目的内容及其目的，同时也能知道他们的信息是如何被使用以及临床医师和患者是如何进对话的信息。

There is another type of consent known as Broad Consent.

另外一种同意书叫广泛同意。

Broad Consent

Broad consent allows participants to give their consent once for their data or tissue to be used in a variety of research studies under certain conditions. Broad consent arrangements are approved by an ethics committee and there should be ongoing governance arrangements in place to ensure that data and/or tissues are used appropriately.

广泛同意是指在某种条件下，患者将自己的信息或者检查组织一次性同意授权给各种研究。广泛同意是通过伦理文员会进行授予的并且在授予过程中一直处于监管状态，确保数据或者组织被合理的使用。

An example of broad consent is often applied to biobanking, a repository of bio samples. The most common justifications for using broad consent is in biobanking are that:

广泛同意的的例子是指那些被应用到生物样品库（用来存放生物样品的地方）。其中一个应用就是生物银行库。

it is impossible and impractical to obtain informed consent for each new research project;
research on biobanks is in the public good as it will bring benefits to all and therefore individual interests can be overridden;
and the research involves minimal risk to individuals because the research focus is on populations and involves limited physical interventions

没有必要每一个新的研究都要去申请患者样品的知情同意，生物银行库是公益项目并能给全人类带来收益，因而个人的权益会被覆盖。这种研究对个人的影响最小因为这种它们是基于群体水平涉及到有限的物理处理。

Why is broad consent so important in research?
Broad consent is often used when setting up research resources, such as biobanks banks, and to allow data from one study to be re-used in future studies. This is important because scientific knowledge is always growing and we often can’t predict what studies might be useful in the future. Broad consent therefore maximises the use of important data and tissue resources in research.

如何让数据更多的被使用，而不是放到故纸堆里面，不过要充分考虑隐私，捐献人的权益以及法律。

There are still huge questions that remain around consent:

要给患者的是什么内容？如何给？

What does a ‘reasonable’ patient want to know?

Are there differences between research and clinical use?
临床和可以对患者样品的使用场景区别是？
Expectations around data sharing. What are they?
什么是数据共享，具体的内容是什么？
Are broad or open consents permissible in law?
广泛同意和开发同意在法律上是同意的么？
